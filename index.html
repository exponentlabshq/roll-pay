Questions

The "wait, how?" section.

So my purchases are actually free sometimes? +

Yes. Every eligible purchase enters a draw. When it wins, Roll pays it off and your receipt shows $0.00. It's discretionary and the odds vary — so enjoy the wins, but never count on a purchase being free.

How is this possible — what's the catch? +

When you pay by card, the network shares a small fee with us. Instead of handing everyone a forgettable 1% back, we pool that and turn it into rare, real "your whole bill is free" moments. No debt, no credit — you only ever spend money you already loaded.

Do I have to spend money to play? +

Never. The Daily Roll lets you play for free every day with no purchase required. "Buy now, pay maybe" is a reward on spending you'd do anyway — not a reason to spend more.

Is my money safe? Can I get it out? +

Roll is self-custodial: you hold the keys, and you can withdraw your digital dollars to your own wallet at any time. We never take custody of your funds.

What's a stablecoin? Is this crypto? +

Your balance is USDC — a digital dollar pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. It's the stable, boring part of crypto: $1 stays $1. We hide all the complicated parts so it just feels like a fast money app.
